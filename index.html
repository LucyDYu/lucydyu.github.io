About

I'm currently a second-year Ph.D. student at Department of Computer Science and Engineering in Chinese Univeristy of HongKong advised by Prof. Irwin King.

My research generally focuses on developing machine learning algorithms applied to graph-structured data. I have worked on developing generalized graph neural networks that are scalable to web-scale datasets, with applications in recommender systems, NLP and IR system. I'm also interested in deep generative models for graphs and privacy-preserved machine learning.

Prior to my PhD, I obtained my M.S. (Computer Science) and B.E. (Electronics Engineering) from the Australia National University and ZhengZhou University, respectively. I have also worked as Senior Applied Machine Learning Engineer at Alibaba Group and JD.com.
